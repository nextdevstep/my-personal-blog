Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 30, 2025

Welcome to Niraj's Modern Blog ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you visit our website, how that information is used, and how it is protected. This policy applies to the website: https://nextdevstep.github.io/my-personal-blog/

1. Information We Collect

Because this is a static website hosted on GitHub Pages, we do not directly collect or store personal data in a database. However, third-party services used for analytics, advertisements, and communication do collect data.

A. Non-Personal Information (Automatic Collection)

  • Log Data: Information like your IP address, browser type (Chrome, Firefox), operating system, pages viewed, time spent on pages, and referring website. This is primarily collected by GitHub Pages and Google Analytics.
  • Device Information: Type of device (desktop/mobile) used to access the blog.

B. Personal Information (Voluntary Submission)

  • Direct Email: If you contact us via the `mailto:` link on our Contact Us page, we receive your name and email address, used solely to respond to your inquiry.
  • Third-Party Contact Form: If we use a form handler (like Formspree), any data you submit is sent to our email and is not stored on our servers.

2. Use of Cookies and Third-Party Advertising

We use technologies, including "cookies," to personalize content, serve ads, and analyze traffic.

A. Google AdSense/Adsterra Disclosure

We seek to monetize this website using third-party advertising networks, including Google AdSense and Adsterra. These partners use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or others (Interest-Based Advertising).

B. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to analyze website use. It uses cookies to gather anonymous information about visitor activity to help us improve content.

C. Affiliate Marketing Links

This blog may contain affiliate links. If you make a purchase through these links, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you, tracked via a cookie.

3. Your Choices Regarding Data

A. Controlling Cookies

You can accept or decline cookies. Most browsers automatically accept them, but you can modify your browser settings to decline. This may affect your user experience.

B. Opting Out of Interest-Based Advertising

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting the Ads Settings page on Google or the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.
